about us

what makes our coffee special?

We only buy in large amounts from reliable farmers and millers. We grade our coffees into different sizes and quality levels, discarding the lower-quality beans, then blind tasting our grades and types to determine the ideal roasting temperature for each size and type of coffee bean – different roasting temps for different coffee. This is something we perform on a regular basis to ensure a consistent and high-quality roast every time.

You may have had to wait a little longer for your coffee, but we guarantee it was the freshest coffee available anywhere.
